National Open University of Nigeria

The University

The National Open University of Nigeria {NOUN} is an open and distance learning {ODL} institute establishedin July 1983 by President Shehu Usman Aliyu Shagari- the first Executive President of Nigeria and the University's founding President. President Chief Olusegun Obasanjo resescited NOUN in 2002. The University is determined to admit and satisfy the education and skill needs of Nigeria no matter their status or circumstances.
